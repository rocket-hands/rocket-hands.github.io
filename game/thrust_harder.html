THRUST HARDER

“Thrust Harder”, by the Kranzky Brothers, published by Arsepipe Productions. The Kranzky Brothers are Lloyd Kranzky (programmer), Keith Kranzky (graphics) and Brian Kranzky (sound effects and music).

The game is a mashup of Thrust, Exile, Mercenary and so forth. You can run around as a little spaceman, in which case you use keyboard to move and mouse to aim and shoot. You can drive a land vehicle, or board a spacecraft to fly around. A little economy based on ore, destructive objects, HP and XP all over the shop, updrades and levelling, a drive to explore, a rescue mission to find all colonists.
